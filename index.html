COMPETITIONS

 

Invest-o-spective

“Invest-O-Spective” is a Venture Capital Case Study Competition organized by Entrepreneurship Cell, IIT Kharagpur in association with Axiomatic Ventures. It allows VC career aspirants to showcase their research and analytical skills. Through Invest-O-Spective, participants get to analyze case studies from Indian tech Startups, explore research and analysis frameworks used by investors, and develop their investment vocabulary.

More info

Business Game

"Business Game" is organised by Entrepreneurship Cell, IIT Kharagpur and includes challenges such as Strategy, Pitching, Marketing, and Negotiation. It provides participants with real-world business experience and improves their thinking ability.

More info

RnD Symposium

RnD Symposium is a unique competition held to mix the 2 fields of entrepreneurship and research and development. The vision is to introduce entrepreneurship to research scholars and Mtech scholars who will be using their research to produce feasible real-life applications and help with their research.

ABOUT US

 

Entrepreneurship Cell, IIT Kharagpur

Entrepreneurship Cell, IIT Kharagpur is the largest student run non profit organization in India, which works with the aim of fostering innovation and entrepreneurship. With 300+ successful startups incubated from IIT Kharagpur in less than 15 years since our inception, we aim at providing students with all the support they require for starting-up, training, mentorship, incubation, patenting, and funding.
